# OptiCare Cold Email Campaign - V1: "The Second Opinion"

## Target Audience
Optometrists, Ophthalmologists, Clinic Owners.

## Strategy
Low friction, high curiosity. Positioning the AI as a "safety net" or "standardized second opinion" rather than a replacement. Asking them to "test" it appeals to their scientific curiosity.

---

## Email 1: The Hook
**Subject:** Second opinion on every fundus image?

Hi {{FirstName}},

I'm building an AI that screens for 8 retinal conditions (DR, Glaucoma, AMD) instantly.

It's not about replacing your diagnosis. It's about having a standardized "second opinion" running in the background for every single patient.

We're in early access. I'm looking for a few innovative ODs to break it and tell us what's wrong.

Open to running a few of your old images through it to see if it catches what you caught?

Best,
Boss

---

## Email 2: The Follow-Up (3 Days Later)
**Subject:** (Re: Second opinion on every fundus image?)

Hi {{FirstName}},

Just bumping this. 

We're testing the sensitivity on Diabetic Retinopathy specifically right now.

If you have any anonymized fundus images you've already graded, I'd love to run them through our model and send you the report.

Worth a quick look?

Best,
Boss
